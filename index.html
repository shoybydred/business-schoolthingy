Production Factors

6 Factors of
Production

Factor 01

Natural Resources

Raw cotton and water used in fabric production — the physical inputs that begin every jersey.

Factor 02

Labour

The skilled workers who cut, sew, and print each jersey by hand and machine.

Factor 03

Capital

The machines, tools, and equipment — sewing machines, cutters, dye printers — used in production.

Factor 04

Information

Customer order data, size specs, design files, and supplier details that guide every production step.

Factor 05

Management

The team that coordinates sourcing, production timelines, quality checks, and delivery.
