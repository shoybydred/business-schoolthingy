Who We Are

Built for Athletes.
Made to Order.

Athletism is a student-run sportswear brand that turns boring school uniforms into personalized, high-performance jerseys. Every single jersey is custom made — no generic stock, no plain designs.

Our jerseys use a 50% polyester / 50% cotton blend with small breathability pores engineered for heat reduction during high-intensity activity.

Available in sizes XS to XXL. Red, white, and black colourways — or request a custom colour for your team.
